Emperor Lounge
The chic centrepiece of Ceilo Exotica’s welcoming and vibrant lobby, our Emperor Lounge is decorated in delicate shades of blue and gold in harmony with our beachfront setting. It’s the perfect spot for meeting up with friends over barista coffees or toasting family celebrations with classic cocktails, fine wines and champagnes. Alongside a menu of savoury bites and tantalizing desserts, the lobby lounge is where you’ll find our ever-popular British Afternoon Tea. Choose from our gourmet tea blends, accompanied by a decadent array of mini sandwiches, baked delicacies and delectable pastries.
