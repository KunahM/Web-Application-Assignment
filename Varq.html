DINING
Varq
Our classy Indian fine-dining establishment was inspired by the Varq, an edible gold or silver leaf that was historically used to decorate meals for Indian royalty. These colors are echoed in the gentle golden tones of the marble floor and the gleaming gold and silver of our custom chandeliers. Our superb food combines time-tested recipes with captivating modern presentations, just as we have given old traditions new vitality. In our display kitchen at Varq, you can see our chefs create exquisite meals, and the handmade drinks and superb wines offer the ideal complement to a wonderful lunch.
