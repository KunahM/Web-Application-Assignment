PALM - KITCHEN
Light and bright, with subtle colours and design, Palm Kitchen sits at the very heart of Taj Exotica, keeping the culinary wheels turning with all-day dining, à la carte menus and buffet extravaganzas. The day’s offerings commence with our sumptuous signature buffet breakfast, piled high with freshly baked bread and pastries, fruits and cereals. Linger over lunch on the sun-dappled terrace, taking a gastronomic journey with enticing global dishes from Milanese risotto to fragrant Thai curries from the à la carte menu. By night, Palm Kitchen transforms into a casual dining destination, where you can indulge in one of the best buffets.
